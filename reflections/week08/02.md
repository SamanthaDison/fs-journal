## Questions

1. Why is communication on a small team important?
Communication is important as it ensures that everyone is involved throughout the development process. This will help reduce unnecessary extra time in meetings.

2. What adjustments from traditional sized scrum teams need to be made to use the agile methodology effectively in a small team?
Having parallel workstreams; with less people it is more likely you will have to have multiple simulataneous streams of development in order to reach the product delivery timeline. This is also why communcation is especially important with smaller development teams.

3. What is the unattainable triangle? What are the tradeoffs of each side?
The unattainable triangle is the paradox of tradeoffs between the speed of development and the quality of the software delivered. 
Good service Cheap won’t be Fast

Good service Fast won’t be Cheap

Fast service Cheap won’t be Good
