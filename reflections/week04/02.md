## Questions

1. What are the three states of a `Promise`?
- 1. Pending: initial state
- 2. Resolved: completed promise
- 3. Rejected: failed promise

2. How do `promise`s seek to resolve the issues of "callback hell"?
Callbacks are attached to promises by returning the following function instead of passing it through. 

3. What is the difference between `.then()` and `.catch()`?
The then() method is called after the promise is resolved. The catch() method is used to 'catch' a failed promise and is usually attached alongside a then().
