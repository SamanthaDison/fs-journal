## Questions

1. What are lifecycle hooks? What are lifecycle hooks used for?
Lifecycle hooks are a window into how the library you’re using works behind-the-scenes. Lifecycle hooks allow you to know when your component is created, added to the DOM, updated, or destroyed.

2. How have you utilized lifecycle hooks in your afternoon projects?
Yes, we have used onMounted hooks to render data to the screen upon loading.

3. What are mounting hooks? When might you use them?
They allow you to access your component immediately before and after the first render; if you need to access or modify the DOM of your component immediately before or after the initial render.
