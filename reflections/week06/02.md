## Questions

1. What are props?
Props are variables that pass information from the parent to its children; similar to how variables are passed into functions as arguments.

2. What are props used for?
Props are used to instantiate modela and components in a dynamic way; pass down properties from a parent to its child.

3. Where can props be used or accessed?
Props are used/accessed with components. Properties or information can be passed through to be instantiated as a 'prop' of a component.
