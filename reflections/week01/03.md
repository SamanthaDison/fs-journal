## Questions

1. What is your favorite tip that you think is the most beneficial to improving your design?
My favorite tip is opting for a box shadow instead of a border. The box shadow is a great tool for separating elements without the harshness of a border. Wireframing prior to building your code is also a great way to improve the overall layout and design of the page.

3. How do you think good design influences people when visiting a website and what sorts of things could you convey through design alone?
Design is integral in providing accessibility; bad design means that some people may not be able to easily navigate the webpage. You can utilize design to place emphasis on primary subjects of a page. People will also be more likely to spend more time on your page if the design is fluid and aestheticall-pleasing.
