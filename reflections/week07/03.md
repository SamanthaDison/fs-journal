## Questions

1. What is a User Experience? What should be considered when developing a User Experience?
User Experience describes the 'experience' customers/clients will have when visiting and interacting with your application. The customer experience should be considered when developing the front-end such as creating things in a way that is clear to the user and will allow the user to perform actions and interact with the application as desired. The user's emotions and feelings we wish to invoke should also be taken into consideration.

2. What are some benefits of taking into account the User Experience?
Taking the UX into consideration can help with the planning process of designing the application. If you understand how you want the user to interact with and feel when using your website, this will provide a clearer roadmap of how to fufill that and plan accordingly.

3. Why is the User Experience important?
UX is important because we want the user to feel satisfied when using the application. In today's age; websites are becoming increasinly important for a business's brand and can be a user/customer's first experience with said company. If a potential customer has a bad UX with the website, they will probably be more likely to take their business elsewhere.
