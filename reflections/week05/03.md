## Questions

1. In simple terms what is timeboxing?
Timeboxing is a time management technique where one assigns a task to be completed during a specific timeframe. 

2. What three strategies will you implement and how?
 - Apply the two-minute rule: don't put off simple tasks that can be completed within a couple minutes.
 - Prioritize: make sure to timebox for tasks that have most importance; for example, focus on functionality and requirements for checkpoints and timebox/prioritize more complex functions.
 - Be flexible: don't give up or become frustrated if certain tasks take longer than the initial timebox. Just create another timebox and move on. 

3. Give an example of when you would have benefitted from timeboxing and how? 
I have used timeboxing when working on checkpoints. I will usually alot a certain amount of time to work on a specific function and be flexible in my timing in case it may take me longer than anticipated.
